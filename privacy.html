Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

AntLabs ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles your information.

What We Collect

AntLabs collects the following data locally on your device only:

— Domain names of websites you visit (not full URLs)
— Sequence and timing of site visits to detect workflow patterns
— Time spent browsing per session
— Automations and preferences you configure

What We Do NOT Collect

— We never collect full URLs, page content, or personal identifiers
— We never collect passwords, form data, or payment information
— We never track you across devices without your consent

How Your Data Is Stored

All behavioral data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cross-device sync, in which case it is encrypted end-to-end and only you hold the keys.

AI Summaries

When you use the Autopilot feature, the content of your open tabs is sent to an AI provider (Groq by default) to generate a summary. This is processed in real-time and not stored by AntLabs. You can use your own API key in Settings for full control.

Third-Party Services

AntLabs optionally integrates with Notion (via your own API token) and AI providers (Groq, OpenAI, Claude, Gemini). Your API keys are stored locally on your device and are never sent to AntLabs servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

Your Rights

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by clicking "Clear All Data" in the Data tab of the extension. Uninstalling the extension also removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

AntLabs is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
