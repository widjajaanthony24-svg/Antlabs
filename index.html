How it works

Three steps.
Zero effort.

Most automation tools make you do the work upfront. AntLabs learns while you work — then does the work for you.

01
👁️

AntLabs watches

Install the extension and browse normally. AntLabs quietly observes which sites you visit, in what order, and at what time — all stored locally on your device.

02
🧠

AntLabs learns

Repeated sequences become patterns. A confidence score builds over time — the more you repeat a workflow, the more certain AntLabs is it's a real habit worth automating.

03
⚡

AntLabs automates

One click converts any detected pattern into an automation. Next time you trigger it, AntLabs opens all your sites automatically — and summarizes everything with AI.
